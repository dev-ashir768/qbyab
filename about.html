The Designer

Aleksandra Baranova is a globally recognized designer and the creative force behind Q. by Aleksandra Baranova. Her relationship with fashion began early; she started her journey as a model at the age of 12, gaining an intimate understanding of how fabric moves on the body and how clothing influences character.

Today, she merges this intuitive knowledge with formal academic rigor. Her multidisciplinary background in fashion design, branding, and visual storytelling has shaped a signature aesthetic where Western structural forms meet the intricate, mindful detailing of the East.

Philosophy & Travel

For Aleksandra, fashion is both art and emotion, crafted with deep intention. Her design approach is heavily influenced by her extensive travels, creating a unique dialogue between cultures.

She views her brand as a platform for awareness. In her own words: "Fashion is a sign language. It comes from the body to the piece of clothing, and then to the huge industry driven by social climate. We should learn to speak it if we want to impact social change."

The Manifesto

“We should learn to speak the language of fashion,
if we want our individuality to be heard
in the massive orchestra of globalization.”
