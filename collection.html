Fashion as Sign Language

Collection

Where Western forms meet Eastern details. Each collection is a narrative, crafted to provoke discussion and reflect the character of the wearer.

Blue Moon
Collection 2023

Inspired by the replication of a woman of herself after wrecking life circumstances, built on the Japanese repair technique “Kintsugi”, where a broken thing is repaired with gold. This metaphor reflects that the value of the woman increases with the birth of a child and each individual challenge she had to endure throughout the lifetime.

“Broken. Repaired in gold. Forged into diamonds.
Every scar is gilded. Every pressure births a diamond.”

Resisting in
Patrician Dream 2024

Based on the contrast of the luxury and restricted working factory wear. During the World Wars the image of a woman and traditional roles were challenged, creating a very interesting blend of the essence turning into diamond of a high value human soul under huge pressure.

“We do not hide the cracks. We fill them with gold.
We do not escape the pressure. We turn it into diamonds.”

Where East craftsmanship meets Western contemporary form wear the statement.
